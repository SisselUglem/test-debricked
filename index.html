Communities are core

The community is the heart of an open source project - health matters.

  • Contributors

    Contributors are the ones with the power to make a project thrive - or die. Make sure to choose projects that are on the rising, not decaying.

  • Security practices

    Processes and habits is what determines the security of a project - and in turn the security of your product. Pick a project that cares.

  • Popularity

    A project used and loved by many is more likely to survive - and excell. Don’t just pick an option that works, pick one that your developes will love.
